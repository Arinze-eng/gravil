# CDN-NETSHARE (CDN) — How to share internet

This app uses a tunnel (Status/Peers dashboard) to connect devices so you can route traffic through the device that has internet.

## Steps

1. **Sign in**
   - Open the app and sign in.

2. **Check access (trial/subscription)**
   - Open **Account** and check **Free access ends**.
   - If your trial has ended, open **Subscription** and complete payment.

3. **Open the dashboard**
   - Go to **CDN dashboard** (Status/Peers).

4. **Start the tunnel (VPN permission)**
   - Tap the **hamburger menu** (top-left).
   - Tap **Start**.
   - If Android asks for VPN permission, tap **Allow**.

5. **Add the other device as a peer**
   - Go to the **Peers** tab.
   - Tap **+** and add the peer.
   - Do the same on the other device so both devices can see each other.

6. **Confirm it’s connected**
   - Go to **Status**.
   - Verify the peer shows as connected/online.

7. **Use the shared connection**
   - Keep the tunnel running on the device that has internet.
   - On the other device, connect through the peer/tunnel path.

## Useful menu items

- **Speed test**: Hamburger menu → *Speed test* (checks your normal internet speed).
- **Restart**: Hamburger menu → *Stop* then *Start* if things feel stuck.

> Note: The exact traffic routing depends on device/network setup, but the tunnel must be running and peers must be connected.
